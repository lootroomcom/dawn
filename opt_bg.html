Features

Everything you need to unleash the full potential of Boosteroid cloud gaming.

01

Smart Device Profiling

Auto-detects CPU cores, RAM, network speed and screen ratio. Classifies your device as Low-End, Mid-Range or High-End for optimal settings.

AI-Powered
02

Smart Resolution Detector

Generates custom resolutions (1080p/2K/4K) aligned to your exact screen ratio: 16:9, 21:9, 32:9 and non-standard displays. Uses native resolution by default.

Ultrawide
03

5 Pro Presets

Default, Cinematic, Competitive, Comfort, Perfect Quality. Each preset tunes filters for different gaming scenarios. All presets are OFF by default.

v3.7.2
04

Advanced Video Filters

USM & CAS sharpening, clarity, denoising, anti-banding, vibrance, gamma, exposure. Organized in Safe, Light, Balanced, Ultra tiers.

8 Filters
05

Adaptive Quality

5 filter tiers (OFF/Safe/Light/Balanced/Ultra) that auto-adjust based on your hardware. Dynamic maxFiltersActive limit per device tier.

Auto-Tune
06

Smart Codec Selector

Probes MediaCapabilities API to auto-select best codec: AV1 > HEVC > VP9 > H.264. Hardware-accelerated decoding preferred.

AV1 Ready
07

Low Latency Mode

Optimizes WebRTC receiver with playoutDelayHint for minimal input lag. Targets 12ms latency with automatic jitter buffer tuning.

Ultra Fast
08

Dashboard Widget

Floating control panel to select your preferred resolution and refresh the page to apply changes. Simple and effective.
